Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SpinZielona collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect basic personal information such as your name, email address, and any data you voluntarily provide when creating an account. We also collect technical data like your IP address, device type, and browser information for security and analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide access to our fantasy football platform, improve user experience, send important notifications, and enhance our services. We may also use non-personal data for analytics and platform optimization.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted service providers who help us operate our website, provided they agree to keep it confidential and secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can also request a copy of the data we hold about you or object to how it's used. To exercise any of these rights, please contact our support team.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

SpinZielona may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage users to review the policy periodically.
