Legal Information

Fantasy Sports and the Law

Fantasy sports are widely considered a game of skill and are legally distinct from gambling. SpinZielona does not offer any form of betting or real-money rewards. Our platform is built for entertainment, competition, and community around football strategy.

Age Restrictions

To ensure compliance with applicable laws and promote responsible usage, SpinZielona is only available to individuals who are 18 years of age or older. By using the site, you confirm that you meet this requirement.

No Real-Money Involvement

Our platform does not include any real-money entry fees, betting mechanics, or cash prizes. SpinZielona is completely free to play and does not facilitate any financial transactions between users or the platform.

Compliance and Transparency

We strive to follow all relevant digital, consumer, and data protection laws. Our policies are publicly available and kept up-to-date. For legal inquiries or questions about our operations, please contact us at support@spinzielona.com.
