Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how SpinZielona uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They help us provide you with a better experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences, keep you signed in, monitor site performance, and understand how users interact with our platform. Some cookies are essential for site functionality, while others are optional.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function properly.
  • Performance cookies: Help us analyze how users navigate the site.
  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences and choices.
  • Analytics cookies: Provide insights for improving the user experience.

Your Choices

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect some parts of our website.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the revised version will be effective immediately upon posting.
